High Pulse Running Club

Number of meetings per week: 2

Price to join: Free

Meeting days: Tuesday, Sunday

Focus: Road running

High Pulse Running Club or Hprunningclub is a running club created by students at the university of Grimstad. The club is open to everyone, regardless of age or running experience. The club has a great mix of beginners and experienced runners. If you want to join the club you can click on the Spond link below to have all the informations about the future runs. For now the club is not organising runs due to the weather in winter but the runs will start again in spring.

Grimstad Running Club

Number of meetings per week: 4

Price to join: 250 NOK

Meeting days: Monday, Tuesday, Thrusday, Saturday or Sunday

Focus: Every distance, mainly marathon distance

The inspiration for this running group comes from the Kristiansand running club, which has been very successful in the last couple of years. The club is open to everyone, regardless of age or running experience. The club has a great mix of beginners and experienced runners.
