UiA Kraken

The university of Agder propose to student the option to study the esport world. As part of a study project some students have created a team at UiA Grimstad to compete in different video games.

Some tournaments are organized by the team at the university and plan to go on national tournaments later. These internal tournaments are open to all students and are a good way to meet new people and have fun. You can either participate as a beginner but also as a more serious player if your objective is to be the best of the best.
